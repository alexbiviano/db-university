1. Contare quanti iscritti ci sono stati ogni anno

Query GROUP BY:
SELECT YEAR(enrolment_date) as year, COUNT(*) as total_enrollments
FROM students 
GROUP BY YEAR(enrolment_date)
ORDER BY year;:
2018	912
2019	1709
2020	1645
2021	734
